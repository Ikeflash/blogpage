Types of Fruits

Apple
A round fruit with red or green skin and a white interior. Apples are sweet or tart in taste.
Banana
A long, curved fruit with yellow skin and soft, sweet flesh inside. Bananas are rich in potassium.
Cherry
A small, round fruit that is typically red or yellow. Cherries are known for their sweet and tart flavors.
Orange
A citrus fruit with a thick, orange rind and juicy, tangy flesh. Oranges are high in vitamin C.
